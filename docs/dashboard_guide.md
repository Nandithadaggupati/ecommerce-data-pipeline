## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Executive Summary
**Purpose**: High-level business overview.
**Key Metrics**: Total Revenue, Total Transactions, Average Order Value, Profit Margin.
**Visualizations**:
- Monthly Revenue Trend (2024 performance)
- Top 5 Categories by Revenue
- Payment Method Distribution (Credit Card vs Cash)

### Page 2: Product Analysis
**Purpose**: Product performance insights.
**Key Insights**: Identifies margin outliers and highest grossing subset categories ensuring fast feedback.

### Page 3: Customer Insights
**Purpose**: Behavior analysis.
**Key Insights**: Maps transaction behavior enabling hyper-segmented marketing strategies.

### Page 4: Geographic & Trends
**Purpose**: Location patterns and weekday correlations.
**Key Insights**: Explores distribution density in cities.

## Filters Available
- Date Range
